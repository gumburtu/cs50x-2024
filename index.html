How to Play:

Select a Difficulty Level:

  • Easy: Guess a number between 1 and 10.
  • Medium: Guess a number between 1 and 100.
  • Hard: Guess a number between 1 and 1000.

Guessing Rules:

  • You have up to 10 turns to find the correct number.
  • After each guess, you'll be informed if your guess is too high or too low.

Good luck, and have fun!

Number Guessing Game

We have selected a random number based on the difficulty level. See if you can guess it in 10 turns or fewer. We'll tell you if your guess was too high or too low.
