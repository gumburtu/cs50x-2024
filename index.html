How to Play:

Welcome to the Number Guessing Game! Here’s how you can play:

Select a Difficulty Level:

  • Easy: Guess a number between 1 and 10. You have 3 guesses.
  • Medium: Guess a number between 1 and 100. You have 5 guesses.
  • Hard: Guess a number between 1 and 1000. You have 10 guesses.

Guessing Rules:

  • Enter a number in the input field and click "Submit Guess".
  • If your guess is correct and you have guesses left, you will see a "Congratulations!" message.
  • If you use all your guesses without finding the correct number, you will see a "Game Over" message.
  • The game will alert you if you enter a number that you have already guessed. Please try a different number.

Additional Notes:

  • Previous Guesses: The game keeps track of all your previous guesses to ensure you do not repeat any.
  • Duplicate Guesses: If you enter a number that you’ve already guessed, the game will warn you and ask you to enter a different number.

Good luck, and have fun!
